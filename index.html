Welcome to 153 Outfitters

Overview

Welcome to 153 Outfitters, where the call of the wild meets the comforts of home. Nestled along the scenic Buffalo National River in Newton County, Arkansas, our mission is to provide you with an unforgettable outdoor experience. Whether you're gliding down the tranquil river in one of our well-maintained canoes or retreating to one of our cozy cabins nestled in nature's embrace, we offer the perfect blend of adventure and relaxation.

Our canoes offer a gateway to exploration, guiding you along the pristine waters of the Buffalo River with ease and expertise. Whether you're an experienced paddler seeking a thrilling adventure or a novice looking to connect with nature, our team is here to ensure your journey is as smooth as the river itself. And when the day's adventures come to a close, our rustic cabins provide a sanctuary where you can unwind and recharge, surrounded by the beauty of the great outdoors.

At 153 Outfitters, we're more than just a destination – we're a state of mind. We invite you to leave behind the hustle and bustle of daily life and immerse yourself in the simple pleasures of nature. Whether you're seeking solace in solitude, forging deeper connections with loved ones, or simply seeking to rediscover the joy of being present in the moment, we're here to help you make the most of your outdoor adventure. Join us at 153 Outfitters, where every moment is an opportunity to embrace the wonders of the natural world and create memories that will last a lifetime.
